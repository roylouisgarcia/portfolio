Just a simple poem
Aint going to erase
Not even the solemn
Empty flower vase
Let the roses grow

Heaven will show
Angels with bows
Pulling their arrows
Please Cupid, aim low
Yes, break this sorrow

Vaults of happiness
Avalanche of wishes
Lollipops and crisps
Every sentimental days
Nights when eyes daze
Til another morning
Initiates mourning
Nothing seems changing
Everythings slowing
Simple poems end.
